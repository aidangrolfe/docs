## Free-Tier Stable Price Odds

Since November 7, 2025, the following endpoints provide free access to de-margined odds with 60-second delay:

- `GET /guest/odds/snapshot/{fixtureId}`
- `GET /guest/odds/stream`

These endpoints only require a JWT token (no subscription needed) and cover the following competitions:

| Competition ID | Competition             | Country      |
|----------------|-------------------------|--------------|
| 7              | Primera División        | Spain        |
| 8              | Premier League          | England      |
| 9              | Bundesliga              | Germany      |
| 10             | UEFA Champions League   | European Cup |
| 13             | Serie A                 | Italy        |
| 16             | Ligue 1                 | France       |
| 18             | UEFA Europa League      | European Cup |
| 26             | Serie A                 | Brazil       |
| 87             | Liga Profesional Argentina | Argentina |

## Odds Channel Content

Fully de-margined stable odds (probabilities) for main markets. Stability comes from the TxODDS stable price product that provides consistent market views, accounting for operators' errors and outages.
