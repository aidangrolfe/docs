## Subscription Model

TxODDS Oracle offers flexible subscription tiers based on your data needs. Subscriptions are purchased on-chain using TxODDS subscription tokens and provide time-limited access to the API.

## Subscription Duration

All subscriptions are purchased in weekly increments. You can choose the number of weeks when subscribing on-chain.

## Service Levels

Different service levels provide varying data access, sampling rates, and league coverage. The pricing matrix defines the cost per week in subscription tokens for each tier.

### Standard Bundle

Standard bundles provide access to predefined league packages with fixed sampling intervals.

### Custom League Selection

Custom league selection allows you to choose specific leagues and competitions for your data feed, with pricing based on your selection.

## Pricing Matrix

The pricing matrix is stored on-chain and defines:
- **Tokens per Week**: Cost in subscription tokens for one week of access
- **Sampling Interval**: How frequently data is updated (in seconds)
- **League Bundle**: Predefined set of leagues (for standard bundles)
- **Market Bundle**: Available market types for odds data

To view the current pricing matrix, query the on-chain `pricingMatrix` account or check during the subscription process.

## How to Subscribe

1. Purchase TxODDS subscription tokens with USDT
2. Choose your service level and duration
3. Execute the `subscribe` transaction on-chain
4. Activate your API token with the off-chain service

See the [Quickstart Guide](/documentation/quickstart) for detailed subscription instructions.
